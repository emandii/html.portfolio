Best movies according to me

My top 3 movies

COCO

A fascinating animated movie;
It's based on core values like family, friendship and courage.

CARS

Another great animated movie, probably the best one yet.
It takes place in a Universe where human kind has gone extinct
and cars took control of the world in the aftermath living human-like lives.

I AM LEGEND

A great film starring Will Smith in the role of "the lonely survivor";
after a virus turned most of the population of New York into monsters
he and his dog are the only ones left to save the human race.
